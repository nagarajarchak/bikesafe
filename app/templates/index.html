What's BikeSafe?

At BikeSafe, we act to promote a safe and sustainable way to cycle around the city of Melbourne and in the process we give back to the environment by promoting a sustainable growth and reduced carbon emissions for the city.

What we stand for?

We believe: Safety > Speed.

We certainly want you to reach your destination quickly, however we want you to do so safely.

What we offer?

We leverage the power of data to offer you insights into the number of bike related accidents that have occurred across the City of Melbourne!

We hope to make your day-to-day bike commute a safer one.
